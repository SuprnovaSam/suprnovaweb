About SuprNova

Our Mission

Founded for Summer 2025, the SuprNova Soccer League exists to bring the energy of pickup soccer into a more structured, reliable format. We offer competitive 4v4 games without the hassle — no chasing down equipment, no fighting for field space, and no putting up with poor sportsmanship. Just show up, play hard, and get better.

What We Offer

  • Fast-paced, small-sided games for more touches and nonstop action
  • Reliable scheduling and secured field space — no guesswork, no stress
  • Simple rules and referees to keep play smooth and competitive
  • High-quality equipment for a better game-day experience
  • A unique competitive table that gives every match something to play for

League Format

Our league table follows the same system as the pros: 3 points for a win, 1 for a draw, and 0 for a loss. Climb the table week by week and fight for the top spot.

The season’s short — but every game counts. Bring the energy, chase the title, and make it matter.
