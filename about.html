About SuprNova

Our Mission

Founded for the summer of 2025. The suprnova soccer league's goal is to provide fun 4v4 soccer games with a structured feel, so you get the best of pick up with out having to worry equipment, feild space, or bad sportsmanship.

What We Offer

  • Small-sided games for better ball touches and more action
  • Reliable scheduling and feild to make your life eaisier
  • Rules and referees to keep the games going
  • Quality equipment to ensure quality gameplay
  • A unique competitive table to bring meaning to the games

League Format

We will have an exiting season with a competitive table. Win games as a team to move up in the table, every game counts! The season is long and victory will be worth playing for.
